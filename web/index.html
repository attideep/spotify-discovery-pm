Terms of use

Bridge Sessions is a free music discovery demo. It is not affiliated with Spotify AB.

You use this app at your own risk. Bridges are suggestions only — always verify artists and content in Spotify.

Do not abuse the API (automated scraping, spam). We may rate-limit abusive traffic.

Privacy

We do not require accounts. Bridge events may be logged anonymously if analytics are enabled.

Preview audio is matched via Apple Music/iTunes search and played in your browser.

Share links encode your intent and track IDs in the URL — only share if you're comfortable.

About playback

Preview clips are ~30 seconds — music licensing limits free full streaming in the browser.

Full songs: Tap Full song to open the track in Spotify.

Volume & EQ: Bass, mid, and treble adjust the preview in real time. The visualizer follows the actual audio.
