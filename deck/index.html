Users don't hate discovery — they hate opaque jumps

"I just replay my 2019 playlists because at least I know what I'm getting."

  • Discovery fatigue (~28%): evaluating new music feels like work
  • Irrelevant recs (~31%): Discover Weekly same vibe weekly
  • Algorithm anxiety (~19%): distrust after bad suggestions

Root cause: no bridge between familiar and novel

  • Recsys optimizes retention → reinforces existing clusters
  • Discovery surfaces are batch-oriented, not moment-aware
  • Jump familiar → novel is too abrupt → skip → comfort retreat
