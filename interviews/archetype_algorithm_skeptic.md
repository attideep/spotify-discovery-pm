# Archetype interview: Algorithm Skeptic

**Profile:** Premium, 31, software engineer. Skips anything labeled recommended.  
**Method:** Synthesized from corpus quotes

---

**PM:** How do you choose what to play?  
**User:** Manually. I distrust the algorithm after it pushed the same artist fifty times because of one accidental play at a party. [^1]

**PM:** Why not use Radio or Discover?  
**User:** Collaborative filtering optimizes retention, not discovery. It's a black box — one weird track and I bail back to albums I know. [^2]

**PM:** What would rebuild trust?  
**User:** Explainability. Tell me why this track bridges from something I like. I'd save more if I understood the logic. [^3]
