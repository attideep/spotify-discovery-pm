# Interview screener

Public form fields (Google Forms / Typeform):

1. Do you use Spotify Premium? (Yes/No) — **Required: Yes**
2. Hours listening per week (1–3 / 4–7 / 8+) — **Target: 5+**
3. What % of listening is from saved playlists or Liked Songs? (<40% / 40–60% / >60%) — **Target: >60%**
4. Rate Spotify's music discovery (1–5) — **Target: 1–3**
5. Email for scheduling (optional)

## Interview guide (20 min)

1. Walk me through your last week of listening — what did you play most?
2. When did you last save a song you hadn't heard before? What triggered it?
3. Tell me about Discover Weekly / Release Radar — what works, what doesn't?
4. What would need to be true for you to try something new during your commute?
5. How do you feel when Spotify recommends something? Do you trust it?
6. If each song came with a one-sentence "why this next" — would that change behavior?

## Research method used in this project

**Corpus triangulation + archetype interviews synthesized from public user voice.**

Live interviews were supplemented by verbatim quotes from App Store, Play Store, Reddit, and HN with source links in `quote_bank.md`.
