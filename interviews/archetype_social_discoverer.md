# Archetype interview: Social Discoverer

**Profile:** Premium, 24, marketing. Finds music via friends, not feeds.  
**Method:** Synthesized from corpus quotes

**PM:** How do you find new music?  
**User:** Friends. Blend and friend activity used to help — social discovery died when the feed went algorithmic. [^1]

**PM:** Why not Spotify's recommendations?  
**User:** Algorithm feels sterile. Friend shares come with trust and context — "you'll like this at our next road trip." [^2]

**PM:** What would Spotify do better?  
**User:** Combine social trust with smart sequencing — not replace friends, but bridge from shared taste. [^3]
